Cookies Policy

What cookies are, the types we use, and how to control them.

General

Welcome to the Cris Vinson cookies policy page.

When you visit our website, our website sends a cookie to your device. We use cookies to improve your experience while on our website. Our cookie policy explains what cookies are, the type of cookies we use, and how we use them. By using crisvinson.com, you consent to the use of cookies.

What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that is sent to and stored in the browser directories of your device (smartphone or PC). Cookies can be used for a variety of purposes such as identifying users, keeping track of preferences and activity. They enable a website to remember information about the user's visit to the site.

Personal Data Under Cookies Statement

Any information that directly, indirectly, or in connection with other information, including a personal identification number, allows for the identification or identifiability of a natural person.

Usage Data Cookies

Information collected automatically through this website includes:

  • IP addresses or domain names of the computers used
  • The URI addresses (uniform resource identifier)
  • The time of the request and method used
  • The size of the file received in response
  • The numerical code indicating the status of the server's answer
  • The country of origin and features of the browser and operating system
  • Various time details per visit and the path followed within the site

Types of Cookies We Use

Necessary Cookies

These are cookies that are essential for the running of our website. Without these cookies, parts of our site would not function.

Functionality Cookies

Functionality cookies are used to remember your preferences on our website and to provide enhanced, more personal features.

Analytics Cookies

Analytical performance cookies are used to monitor the performance of our website, for example, to determine the number of page views and the number of unique users.

Surveys Related Cookies

From time to time, we offer user surveys and questionnaires. These surveys may use cookies to remember who has already taken part.

Forms Related Cookies

When you submit data through a form, cookies may be used to remember your user details for future correspondence.

Essential Cookies

We may use essential cookies to authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts.

Third-Party Cookies

We may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the site and deliver advertisements.

Advertising Cookies

We use adverts to offset the costs of running this site. Behavioral advertising cookies are designed to ensure that we provide you with the most relevant adverts where possible.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

If you'd like to delete cookies or instruct your web browser to delete or refuse cookies, please visit the help pages of your web browser. Please note that if you delete cookies or refuse to accept them, you might not be able to use all of the features we offer.

Most browsers allow you to block cookies. In Internet Explorer, you can block all cookies by clicking "Tools > Internet Options > Privacy." In Firefox, you can adjust your cookies settings by clicking "Tools > Options > Privacy."

Do-Not-Track Signals

We currently do not employ technology that recognizes "do-not-track" signals from your browser.
