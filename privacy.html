Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, store, and share your personal information.

1. Overview

Welcome to the Cris Vinson Privacy Policy Page.

This Privacy Policy explains how we (crisvinson.com) collect your data, how we process them, how we secure them, how we might share them as regards to providing our services, and how we store them. This Privacy Statement also describes what rights you have regarding your personal data, how you give your consent, and how you withdraw your consent.

2. Acceptance and Use

By using our website, you signify acceptance of the terms of this Privacy Policy. When you click the accept button on the site, this is considered your official consent to process your personal information. If you do not agree with any aspect of this Privacy Policy, you should immediately discontinue access or use of the website.

3. Data Protection Principles

Personal data means any information that enables us to identify you, directly or indirectly, such as name, email, address, telephone number, or any form of identification number.

We are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws and will ensure that personal data is:

  • Used lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent way
  • Collected only for valid purposes that we have clearly explained to you
  • Relevant to the purposes we have told you about and limited only to those purposes
  • Accurate and kept up to date
  • Kept only as long as necessary for the purposes we have told you about

4. Personal Information We Collect About You

The information you provide to us is considered personally identifiable information, which may include your name, address, email address, telephone number, and any other information that can identify you.

5. Types of Data We Collect

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Full name, email address
  • Financial Information: Credit card and billing information, payment card primary account number (PAN), transaction history
  • Correspondence: Survey responses, information provided to our support team

6. Non-Personal Information

Non-personal information refers to information that, by itself, does not identify you as a specific individual (e.g., demographic information or website visitations). We may collect non-personal information automatically through the use of industry-standard technologies.

7. Information Collected Through Technology

While you visit our site, we or our third-party service providers may collect non-personal information about your use of the website (e.g., your internet browser, operating system, IP address, connection speed). This is collected through:

  • Cookies: Small pieces of information stored by your browser on your device
  • IP Address: A number automatically assigned to your computer by your ISP
  • Beacons: Small pieces of data embedded in web pages and emails

8. Payment Processor

Payments made on our website are processed through our designated payment gateway provider (e.g., PayPal or Stripe). You provide credit or debit card information directly to them. Your payment card details are not shared with us by the provider.

9. How We Use Your Data

We may use your data for the following purposes:

  • Identification and authentication
  • Communicating with you
  • Improving our services
  • Customizing your experience
  • Network and data security
  • Marketing and advertising
  • Exercising our legal rights
  • Legal compliance
  • Research and development

10. With Whom We Share Your Data

We may share data with third parties as follows:

  • Authorized vendors: Third-party vendors that help us operate our website and process payments
  • Advertising: Advertising companies to display relevant ads to you
  • Analytics: Persons who provide analytics showing how customers use our services
  • Affiliates and advisors: Our auditors and advisors for planning, financial reporting, and legal compliance
  • Certain legal situations: Where we believe disclosure is necessary to comply with a legal obligation

11. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use our services. When you stop using our service and unsubscribe, we will delete your personal information. We may keep logs of automatically collected information, your email address, tax information, communications with you, and transactional information for auditing, tax, and commercial purposes.

12. Your Privacy Choices

  • You may choose not to provide us with certain information
  • You may change your privacy settings
  • You may opt-out of receiving commercial emails from us
  • You may limit the use of cookies

13. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998 (COPPA) and GDPR. We do not knowingly collect personal information from any person under the age of 13.

14. Cross Border Transfers

To facilitate our global operations, we may transfer, store, and process your information within our family of companies, partners, and service providers based throughout the world. By communicating electronically with us, you acknowledge and agree to the processing of your personal information in this way.

15. Security Measures

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal information. Please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

16. Email Communications

By using our website and services, you consent to receive commercial emails from us. All users may decline to receive commercial messages. Please note that any opt-out request may take several days to process.

17. Your Right to Withdraw Consent

You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us at cvinson@funnelprimo.com. You can also change your marketing preferences.

18. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We continually strive to implement new technologies and processes to better protect your privacy. Changes to this privacy policy may be made from time to time. In no event will these changes result in the degradation of any of the security measures designed to protect you.

19. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: cvinson@funnelprimo.com
