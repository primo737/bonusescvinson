Mechanics, plainly
What counts as a share?
A real human who signs up through your link before the workshop ends Saturday. Duplicates from the same email and self-signups don't count.
When do my bonuses unlock?
The moment your count crosses the threshold. VIP bundle at 5, Civy Prompt Pack at 10, signed book at 20. The premium prizes — coaching call, custom LaunchMap, and free website — go to the top 3 referrers with 20 or more shares, announced live during the workshop on Saturday.
How are the top 3 picked?
Pure share count. Whoever brings the most registrants through their link wins. You need 20 or more shares to qualify. Ties are broken by who hit 20 first. The leaderboard is visible in your portal so you always know your standing.
Where do I find my link?
It's in the share row above and inside your portal. The portal also shows your live share count, which tiers you've already unlocked, and your current rank if you're chasing top 3.
What if I share and nobody signs up?
You keep your free seat. The bonuses are pure upside. The workshop itself is free either way.
