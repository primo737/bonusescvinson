Section 04 · Quick answers

Before you register, what you need to know.

04 / 04 sections
What exactly will I walk out with?
One published asset (offer page, lead magnet, FAQ, or sales email, your choice). One Skill that handles a recurring task in your business every week starting Monday. And three use cases you can copy from founders in your industry. Not handouts. Real things, running in your Claude account when you go to bed Saturday.
Do I need to know how to code?
No. This workshop is built for founders, not developers. Zero code. Zero terminal. If you can write an email, you can do everything we cover.
I have never used Claude. Will I keep up?
Yes. This is built for first-time users. You will leave with Claude set up, your business loaded in, and a clear next step. No prior experience needed.
Is this really free?
Yes. The online seat is free. We make it that way so the founders who need this most can show up.
What do I need to bring?
Your laptop. One real piece of your business you want to ship. One real bottleneck you have been stuck on. Bring those two things and we work on them live.
Will there be a chance to ask questions?
Yes. Q&A runs throughout, not just at the end. Online attendees ask in chat. In-person attendees ask out loud. Cris answers using your actual business as the example.
